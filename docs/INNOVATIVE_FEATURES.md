**Concept**: Automatically adapt the app's mood and features based on time, cultural events, and user activity.

### 2. **Moment Capsules** - Time-Released Memories

### 3. **Coffee Break Digest** - Smart Content Curation
**Concept**: AI-curated 5-minute content packages delivered at your coffee/tea time.

**Features**:

- Algorithm picks: 2 friend updates, 1 trending reel, 1 local event, 1 creator highlight
- "Pause and Resume" - pick up where you left off
- Zero-scroll anxiety: Fixed content, no infinite feed
- Egyptian context: Aligns with ahwa (coffee shop) culture
